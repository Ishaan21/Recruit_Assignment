The Solution

A remedy for this is to use seed paper. This is a biodegradable paper embedded with plant and flower seeds. The purpose is not only to recycle, but to have a safe alternative when discarding your used paper. It can be planted in a flower pot or in your garden for growing. This miraculous green paper can add beauty and charm to your home or even your office flower pots.
